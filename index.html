Being a user is like you can have tickets to The Wizarding World of Harry Potter, but being a developer gives you a real wand.

So here I am, flying 7243 miles away from home, pursuing my Master's degree. Because I believe only through technology can we democratize education, finance, and everything else. I also believe technology is the key to the future, and I wish to hold this key.
